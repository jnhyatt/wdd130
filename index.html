Utah

I'm from Utah where the air is dry and thin. Utah is home to several national parks and there's plenty to do if you love being outside. The Salt Lake Valley is a busy and rapidly-growing collection of cities and suburbs, but further from this epicenter you can find vast swaths of farmland. Utah exports a wide variety of agricultural products and is named the Beehive State after one of its core values – industry.
